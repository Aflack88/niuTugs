About NIU Tugs

The Tradition

NIU Tugs is a long-standing tug-of-war competition deeply rooted in Northern Illinois University's Greek Life. Teams compete from trenches in an intense display of strength, strategy, and teamwork.

Brotherhood & Sisterhood

More than just a competition, Tugs builds lasting bonds within fraternities and sororities. The weeks of training and competition create unforgettable memories and strengthen Greek community ties.

Philanthropy

NIU Tugs raises significant funds for charitable causes, combining athletic competition with community service and making a real difference in the lives of others.

How It Works

  • Teams of 8 pullers compete from opposing trenches
  • Matches consist of multiple pulls with point-based scoring
  • Annual tournament features multiple divisions and brackets
  • Winners advance through elimination rounds to championship
